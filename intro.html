Will It Land?

HOW IT WORKS

Your goal is to land the rocket safely and vertically. You can set the rocket's initial velocity, and some other parameters. Then, you must decide in advance which altitude your motor should restart, how long it should run for, and how much thrust it should give.
If your rocket doesn't land safely, it will explode! Your astronauts will have died and it's all your fault.
Good luck!
